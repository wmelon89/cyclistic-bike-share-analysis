# Cyclistic Bike Share Analysis

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate Capstone Project.

The objective was to analyze the differences between annual members and casual riders and provide recommendations to increase annual memberships.

## Business Task

How can Cyclistic convert casual riders into annual members?

## Tools Used

- R
- RStudio
- tidyverse
- lubridate

## Data Source

Divvy Trip Data (2025)

## Key Findings

1. Annual members took more rides than casual riders throughout the year.

2. Both annual members and casual riders reached a peak in usage in August. However, the number of rides by casual riders dropped sharply after August.

3. Casual riders spent more time per ride than annual members.

4. Annual members used bikes more frequently on weekdays, while casual riders used bikes more frequently on weekends.

## Recommendations

1. Promote annual memberships to frequent casual riders with incentives, including volume discounts for rides.

2. Promote annual memberships during the summer season and weekends because casual rider activity is at its peak during these periods.
